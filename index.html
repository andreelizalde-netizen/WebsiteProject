Six Flags Magic Mountain

About Magic Mountain

Six Flags Magic Mountain is an amusement park located in Valencia, California. Known for its thrilling rides and attractions, it is home to some of the tallest and fastest roller coasters in the world. The park opened in 1971 and has since become a popular destination for thrill-seekers and families alike.

Magic Mountain features a range of entertainment options, including live shows, games, and seasonal events, making it a versatile attraction for visitors of all ages.

Attractions

  • Full Throttle

Features

The park spans over 260 acres and offers more than 19 roller coasters, along with various rides and water attractions. Its unique landscape includes thrilling drop towers and family-friendly attractions. The park's vibrant atmosphere is complemented by themed areas, dining options, and shops throughout the premises.

Dining Options

Magic Mountain offers a variety of dining options to cater to different tastes. Visitors can enjoy everything from quick snacks to full meals at restaurants like Panda Express, Johnny Rockets, and various food stands throughout the park.
